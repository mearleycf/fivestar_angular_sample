Business Owner's Policy

Why we recommend it

Some risks fall outside the protection of general liability coverage. That’s where a Business Owner’s Policy (BOP) can help. It protects your commercial buildings and most of your personal property. And in the event of lost business, it could help you pay for lost income, employee salaries, and rent expenses if you have a claim.

What it covers

  • Bodily injury & property damage
  • Personal & advertising injury
  • Products & completed operations
  • Medical bills
  • Building coverage
  • Personal property/contents*
  • Business income
  • Personal property off premises
